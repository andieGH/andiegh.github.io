The Owners

Andie Green-Harrison

Andie has a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Calgary. She worked as a consultant for several years, working on project across Canada and the United States. During her time as a consultant she had the opportunity to work with a wedding planning company. She enjoyed the creativity of planning events and loved working with people to acheive their dream weddings. In 2015, and decided to start her own wedding planning company, and Petals and Peonies was created.





Holly Clayton

Holly has a BSc Eng in Civil and Environmental Engineering from the Univeristy of Alberta. She began her career as a civil engineer working for a firm in Ontario. Holly enjoys the fast paced environment that her work provides. She continues to excel in this role part time after parterning with Andie on Petals and Peonies in 2016. Holly brings her keen eye for design and her uplifting spirit to every wedding she is a part of.
